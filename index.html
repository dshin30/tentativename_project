What is PM2.5?

PM2.5 refers to particulate matter smaller than 2.5 micrometers. These particles enter deep into the lungs and bloodstream.

Traffic Volume vs PM2.5

To evaluate whether traffic volume influences air quality, we compared NYC traffic counts with PM2.5 levels. The correlation was found to be extremely weak (r ≈ –0.02), indicating traffic is not the primary driver of PM2.5 changes.
